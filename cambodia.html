About Cambodia

Cambodia, steeped in ancient history and adorned with magnificent temples, invites you to delve into its cultural riches. Marvel at the architectural wonders of Angkor Wat, stroll through the bustling markets of Siem Reap, and cruise along the tranquil waters of Tonle Sap Lake. With its warm hospitality and rich heritage, Cambodia promises an unforgettable journey through time.

Must-Visit Destinations

  • Angkor Wat
  • Bayon Temple
  • Ta Prohm Temple
  • Phnom Penh Royal Palace
  • Koh Rong Island

Things to Do

  • Explore ancient temples and ruins
  • Take a boat tour on Tonle Sap Lake
  • Visit traditional floating villages
  • Experience Khmer cuisine
  • Enjoy traditional Apsara dance performances

Local Cuisine

Cambodian cuisine is a blend of flavors influenced by neighboring countries. Some popular dishes include:

  • Amok (fish or chicken curry)
  • Bai Sach Chrouk (pork and rice)
  • Kuy Teav (noodle soup)
  • Num Banh Chok (rice noodles with fish curry)
  • Banana Blossom Salad

Travel Tips

  • Respect local customs and traditions, especially when visiting temples.
  • Carry small bills for tipping and purchasing goods from local vendors.
  • Stay hydrated and drink bottled water.
  • Wear modest clothing, especially when visiting religious sites.
  • Be cautious of scams and touts, especially in tourist areas.
