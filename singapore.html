About Singapore

Singapore, a modern metropolis with a rich cultural heritage, offers a harmonious blend of tradition and innovation. Explore the vibrant neighborhoods of Chinatown, Little India, and Kampong Glam, each offering its own unique charm and flavor. Indulge in world-class shopping and dining experiences, stroll along the picturesque waterfront of Marina Bay, and discover the hidden gems of this dynamic city-state.

Must-Visit Destinations

  • Marina Bay Sands
  • Gardens by the Bay
  • Sentosa Island
  • Orchard Road
  • Singapore Zoo

Things to Do

  • Experience the world's largest observation wheel at the Singapore Flyer
  • Explore the vibrant street food scene at hawker centers
  • Visit iconic landmarks such as the Merlion and the Esplanade
  • Shop till you drop on Orchard Road
  • Enjoy a leisurely walk or bike ride along the Southern Ridges

Local Cuisine

Singaporean cuisine is a melting pot of flavors influenced by Chinese, Malay, Indian, and Western traditions. Some popular dishes include:

  • Hainanese Chicken Rice
  • Chilli Crab
  • Laksa
  • Hokkien Mee
  • Satay

Travel Tips

  • Respect local customs and traditions, especially in places of worship.
  • Be mindful of strict laws regarding littering, smoking, and chewing gum.
  • Stay hydrated and protect yourself from the sun, especially in the tropical climate.
  • Use public transportation, such as the efficient MRT system, to get around the city.
  • Enjoy the diverse cultural events and festivals throughout the year.
