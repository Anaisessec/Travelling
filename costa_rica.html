About Costa Rica

Costa Rica, nestled between the Pacific Ocean and the Caribbean Sea, is renowned for its breathtaking biodiversity and eco-tourism opportunities. Explore its dense rainforests, towering volcanoes, and pristine beaches. From zip-lining through the lush canopies to surfing along the sun-kissed shores, Costa Rica offers an adventure for every nature enthusiast.

Must-Visit Destinations

  • Monteverde Cloud Forest Reserve
  • Manuel Antonio National Park
  • Arenal Volcano
  • Tortuguero National Park
  • Corcovado National Park

Things to Do

  • Zip-lining through the rainforest canopy
  • Hiking to hidden waterfalls
  • Surfing on the Pacific coast
  • Wildlife spotting in national parks
  • Relaxing on pristine beaches

Local Cuisine

Costa Rican cuisine is a reflection of its diverse ecosystems and cultural influences. Some popular dishes include:

  • Gallo Pinto (rice and beans)
  • Casado (traditional meal with rice, beans, plantains, salad, and meat)
  • Ceviche (fresh seafood marinated in lime juice)
  • Tamales (steamed corn dough filled with meat and vegetables)
  • Guanabana (tropical fruit)

Travel Tips

  • Learn some basic Spanish phrases.
  • Bring lightweight, breathable clothing for warm weather.
  • Apply sunscreen and insect repellent.
  • Respect local customs and traditions.
  • Stay hydrated and drink bottled water.
